Easy setup

Dozer brings you the power of BOSH at the click of a button. We can deploy a fully operational BOSH for you within 30 seconds, together with the Cloud Provider Interface (CPI) you need. Getting a new BOSH to deploy a new infrastructure should be a hassle-free experience. With Dozer this is guaranteed.

Fully managed

We believe (cloud) operators should be able to focus on creating and / or deploying BOSH releases without having to manage yet another distributed system. Dozer takes care of everything on the BOSH side of things, keeping BOSH, all of its dependencies and the underlying infrastructure running and up to date.

Enhanced UX

Our software comes with a couple of very useful power-ups for BOSH, such as central user management (Oauth) and a GUI for BOSH including VM overviews and task log visualization that links tasks to users. Dozer aims to give operators a bird's eye view across all of their BOSH deployments.




About

Dozer is all about onboarding BOSH, the best cloud orchestration and lifecycle management tool for the cloud! Dozer makes BOSH available on-demand and enables you to manage multiple BOSHes with multiple different purposes. Therefore it is truely the multi-cloud solution everyone is looking for!
